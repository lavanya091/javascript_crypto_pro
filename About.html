About CoinGecko

CoinGecko is a comprehensive cryptocurrency data platform that provides real-time price information, market capitalization, trading volume, and other key metrics for thousands of cryptocurrencies.

Our mission is to empower users with the information they need to make informed decisions in the fast-paced world of cryptocurrencies. We aim to provide transparency in the cryptocurrency market by offering unbiased data and resources.

Features

- Real-Time Data: Access live price updates for cryptocurrencies across various exchanges.

- Market Cap Rankings: Discover the top cryptocurrencies by market capitalization.

- Portfolio Tracking: Track your cryptocurrency holdings and performance over time.

- News & Insights: Stay updated with the latest news, trends, and insights in the crypto space.
